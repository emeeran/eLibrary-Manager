About eLibrary Manager

eLibrary Manager

PRO
Version: 1.0.0
Build: 2025.02.08

A lightweight web application for managing and reading large ebook collections with AI-powered chapter summarization. Designed as a pixel-perfect digital twin of Icecream eBook Reader Pro 6.53.

Features

  • Support for EPUB, PDF, and MOBI formats
  • AI-powered chapter summaries
  • Text-to-speech support
  • Multiple reading themes (Day, Sepia, Night)
  • Progress tracking and bookmarks
  • Full-text search
